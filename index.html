How We Will Test Our Hypothesis

🐀

Animal Model

“We will use the TgF344-AD transgenic rat and compare to WT rats at 6 age points (3–19 months).”

🧭

Behavioral Testing

“Assess long-term spatial memory (Barnes Maze) and working memory (Y-Maze).”

🧠

Neuropathological Assessment

“Quantify TrkA:p75NTR ratio on cholinergic neurons and assess Aβ plaque deposition.”

Anticipated Outcomes

“We expect a significant decrease in the TrkA:p75NTR ratio in AD rats as they age.”
This molecular change is expected to correlate with cognitive decline measured in the behavioral tests.
“This would support the hypothesis that impaired neurotrophic support contributes to AD pathogenesis.”

Why This Matters

“This research could provide deeper insights into AD mechanisms and support targeted therapies modulating neurotrophin signaling in early stages.”

“The data could fuel computational models to simulate interventions to predict impacts on neuronal survival.”
